Frequently Asked Questions

Can your staff help choose the perfect dumpster for my project?

Yes, our staff is happy to assist in selecting the best dumpster for your project. We consider the type of waste you need to dispose of, the weight of the materials, and the accessibility of the location to ensure we recommend the right dumpster for your needs.

Do you offer pickup service?

Definitely! We not only deliver dumpsters to your site but also ensure that the waste is disposed of in an eco-friendly manner, helping to keep your community clean and sustainable.

Do you accept all types of waste?

No, we only accept non-hazardous waste. We do not accept hazardous waste, which includes combustible waste, corrosive waste, reactive waste, toxic waste, biohazardous waste, and radioactive waste.

How do you ensure waste is disposed of in an eco-friendly manner?

We dispose of waste in an eco-friendly manner by reducing the amount of waste that ends up in landfills, thereby helping to reduce carbon emissions. We achieve this by donating usable materials and recycling waste whenever possible.

Does your rental fee cover both delivery and pickup?

Yes, it does. Our rental fee includes both the delivery and pickup of the dumpster. We handle waste disposal responsibly and take care to protect your property during delivery and removal.

Do I need to dispose of the waste myself after filling the dumpster?

No, you don’t need to worry about disposal. Your rental fee includes both delivery and pickup of the dumpster.
